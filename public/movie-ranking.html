Tyler's Favorite Movies

Just a small list of some of my favorite movies

Trilogies

Back to the Future

This series is, in my opinion, the perfect trilogy. The three movies reference each other consistently, show character growth, role reversal, and goofy script writing. I love the characters, the plot, the action, and the rewatchability.

Men in Black

Funny alien movies that I often rewatch. Will Smith does a great job, and the aliens are interesting.

Spiderverse

Please let part 3 come out soon! Spiderverse is my favorite rendition of the spiderman movies. They are charming, have a wide, varied cast of characters, and have a great sense of humour.


Mysteries

Clue

The classic murder mystery. Hilarious writing and iconic soundtrack.

Knives Out

An intriguing spin on the classic murder mystery. It left me suspenseful in the first half of the movie, and shocked in the second half.
